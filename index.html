Technology

My technical interests are centered on building useful AI systems that are reliable, explainable, adaptable, and practical enough for real-world engineering use.

01
LLM-based software defect prediction

Building systems that combine document retrieval, embeddings, vector search, and language models to improve factual grounding and reduce hallucination.
